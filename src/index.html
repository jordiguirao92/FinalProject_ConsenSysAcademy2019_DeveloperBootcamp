What is CITYBetter?

CITYBetter is a dApp where citizens can create a request to improve the city environment. For example, you can create a request to ask the city council to fix a traffic light.
Other example, you have seen a tree disrepair you request to the city council that provide a solution. The city council will manage your request, if it consider that your request is needed and important, the council will resolved it. After that, you will receive the CYT (City Token) reward. The citizen can spend these tokens in supermarkets, pharmacy, fuel station, etc of the city.
